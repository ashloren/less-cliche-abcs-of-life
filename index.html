B is for Beauty.
You possess a variable amount of two different types of beauty: inner & outer.

Your inner beauty can last throughout life, if you work to keep it intact.

Your outer beauty, however, will eventually fade.

It is yours on lease, with no option to buy.

F is for Freedom.
Perhaps the single most valuable commodity in life.

The most commonly held belief is that this award goes to Time.

Of course, we each have a finite amount of time to be alive.

But, what if that were not the case?

Does immortality seem desirable in the absence of freedom?

Ultimately, freedom = possibilities.

H is for Hatred.
An intense, all-consuming aversion to someone or something.

It forms an endpoint on the spectrum of human emotion.

You can think of it like the top of the food pyramid.

As with sugar, butter & salt: do your best to use sparingly.

J is for "Jack-of-all-Trades Syndrome."
Long-term lack of specialization.

A phrase I made up, used to describe a person like myself who has never (and still probably doesn't) really know what they want to do with their lives.

People suffering from this condition often make it all the way through life without declaring a Major (much to their disadvantage, in most cases).

The result?

Earning a degree in, well...nothing.


K is for Knowledge.
All things which are known by an individual.

Clustered information in our minds that amount to our skills and abilities.

The human brain is estimated to have a data storage capacity of around 2.5 petabytes, although we have no reliable method for calculating the sizes of the memories stored within them.

Thankfully, our disc is rewritable.

N is for Nostalgia.
Involuntary time travel.

Activated by anything familiar that has been inextricably associated with your memories of the past.

Depending on the trigger, you may travel back to: an entire era of your life, a significant event within it, or (at its most granular) a specific interaction.

Reliably produces strong feelings of sentimentality.

Ends abruptly and often causes intense yearning to remain in the memory.


O is for Orgasm.
A crescendo of physical pleasure brought about by genital stimulation.

At first, they are a gift you will mostly be giving to yourself.

Later on and with some luck, others will start giving you some too.

Do NOT be a greedy hoarder of orgasms!

Giving them is *almost* as fun as receiving.
